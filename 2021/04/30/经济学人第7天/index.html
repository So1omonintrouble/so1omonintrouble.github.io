  3. The experiences of covid-19 and Mr Trump have formed views in young Chinese that will linger long after both are gone. Mr Trump threatened to ban Huawei, a national champion; TikTok, a winning export; and WeChat, a lifeline to family back home for those overseas. A popular joke online is that Mr Trump encouraged the Chinese to rally round the flag.
    新冠肺炎和特朗普的经历在中国年轻人中形成的观念在消失后仍将长期存在。特朗普威胁要禁止华为，中国龙头企业，抖音，一个成功的出口产品;微信，可以让那些在海外的人与家人联系的生命线。网上一个流行的笑话是，特朗普鼓励中国人团结在国旗周围。

    rally 团结

  4. The Tata name thus pervades all aspects of Indian life. An outside appraisal cited by Tata Sons values the brand, for the use of which the parent charges affiliates a royalty, at $20bn. That makes it Tata Sons’ second-most-valuable asset behind only its $89bn stake in TCS. But the lattice of business, do-goodery and trust, all wrapped up in a beloved brand, now faces problems of its own, from inside its corporate structure and from stiffer competition beyond it.
    塔塔这个名字因此遍及印度生活的方方面面。塔塔之子引用的一份外部评估对该品牌进行了估价，母公司向子公司收取200亿美元的使用费。这使其成为塔塔之子仅次于其在TCS 890亿美元股份的第二大最有价值资产。但是商业、行善和信任的格子，都包裹在一个受人喜爱的品牌中，现在面临着自己的问题，来自公司内部结构和更激烈的竞争。

    charge affiliates a royalty 向子公司收取版税

    lattice 格子

    stiffer 激烈的

  5. But the pandemic has shown just how urgent reform is.     lambast 猛烈抨击
