  2. Mr Miller boasted that his firm sealed a wellbore in the North Sea with cement in a process that was, for the first time, fully automated.
    米勒先生炫耀道，他的公司在北海首次以完全自动化的方式用水泥密封了一个钻井孔。

    wellbore 钻井孔

    cement 水泥，巩固，加强

  3. A career retrospective that filled the galleries at Tate Britain in 2017 (before a worldwide tour) brought home how much work she had made over 30 years, freeing her to set off in a new direction.
    2017年，泰特英国美术馆举办的一场职业回顾展(在全球巡演之前)使她深刻认识到她30多年来所做的工作，让她可以自由地走向新的方向。

    retrospective 回顾的，怀旧的，可追溯的

  4. A scheme to trace supply chains known as ITSCI run by the International Tin Association based in London and an American charity, Pact, helped bring tentative buyers back to Congo.
    总部设在伦敦的国际锡业协会和美国慈善机构Pact经营的名为ITSCI的供应链追踪计划，帮助将暂定的买家带回刚果。

    a scheme to trace supply chains 供应链追踪计划

    tentative 试验性的，暂定的，踌躇的

    ITSCI官网

    ITSCI works to achieve avoidance of conflict financing, human rights abuses, or other risks such as bribery in mineral supply chains. Our reference is the OECD Due Diligence Guidance and the OECD has confirmed our standards are 100% aligned with the guidance; see here.
    Our monitored supply chains allow metal users to source responsibly and avoid total disengagement from high risk areas, while ensuring that miners continue to benefit from responsible business opportunity.
    By making available unique and credible information to businesses, government authorities and civil society participation in ITSCI allows them to take appropriate and effective decisions on due diligence and steps towards progressive improvement.
    Sharing of our detailed and credible information, which is generated to a common standard by a range of appropriate experts, encourages accountability of all stakeholders, and as a result, reduces business risk and increases opportunity for all.
    ITSCI currently focuses on Burundi, Democratic Republic of Congo, Rwanda and Uganda.

    ITSCI致力于避免冲突融资、侵犯人权或其他风险，如矿产供应链中的贿赂。我们的参考是OECD尽职调查指南，OECD已确认我们的标准与指南100%一致；看这里。

    我们监控的供应链允许金属用户负责任地采购，避免完全脱离高风险地区，同时确保矿工继续受益于负责任的商机。

    通过向企业提供独特和可信的信息，政府当局和民间社会参与ITSCI使它们能够就尽职调查和逐步改进的步骤作出适当和有效的决定。

    分享我们的详细和可信的信息，这些信息是由一系列适当的专家按照共同的标准生成的，鼓励所有利益相关者承担责任，从而降低业务风险，增加所有人的机会。

    ITSCI目前的工作重点在于布隆迪、刚果民主共和国、卢旺达和乌干达。

    ingrained 根深蒂固的

    political meddling 政治干预

    oligarch 寡头政治的执政者
