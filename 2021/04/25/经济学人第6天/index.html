经济学人第6天
So1omon Lv3
 2021-04-25 15:15:15  
  • 英语 
 
  • 经济学人 
 

  1. Misaligned incentives and poor monitoring often cause costs to swell. Nine out of ten mega-projects overrun their budget and schedule—by an average 70% and 61%, respectively.
    不合理的激励和糟糕的监督往往会导致成本膨胀。十个大型项目中有九个超出了预算和计划，平均分别达到70%和61%。

    misaligned 方向偏离的，不重合的，未对准的，线向不正的

    overrun 泛滥，超过，蹂躏

  2. Convergence in incomes between poor states and rich ones, so rapid before 1980, has slowed dramatically since, and the productivity gap between superstar cities and others has yawned.
    在1980年之前，贫穷国家和富裕国家之间的收入收敛如此之快，但自那以后，这种差距大幅放缓，超级明星城市和其他城市之间的生产率差距也在拉大。

    convergence 收敛，会聚，集合

    dramatically 大幅度

    yawn 拉开

  3. The Corpus Rubenianum, for instance, is an Antwerp based body that adjudicates the work of Peter Paul Rubens; it reveres the legacy of Ludwig Burchard, a German-born expert who died in 1960.
    例如，总部位于Antwerp的Rubenianum语料库负责裁定Peter Paul Rubens的作品;它尊重Ludwig Burchard的遗产，这位出生于德国的专家于1960年去世。

    corpus 语料库

    语料库指经科学取样和加工的大规模电子文本库，其中存放的是在语言的实际使用中真实出现过的语言材料。

  4. They have been plying the coastline in search of “black gold”. Not oil, but the sea cucumber, a large slug-like creature that infests the ocean floor. Local fisher men have traditionally ignored them, since locals deem them unappetising.

    他们一直在这条海岸线上往返寻找“黑金”。不是油，而是海参，一种生活在海底的类似鼻涕虫的大型生物。当地渔民在传统上忽视了它们，因为当地人对它们没有胃口。

    ply 使用，不住地使用，折，弯，从事

  5. Yet his administration housed appointees whose fervent support of democratic Taiwan is the flipside of their Manichaean view of the evils of China.
    然而，他的政府中有一些被任命的人，他们对民主台湾的热情支持恰恰相反于他们对中国邪恶的二元论的观点。
