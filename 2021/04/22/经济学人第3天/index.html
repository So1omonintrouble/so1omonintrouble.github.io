  1. It felt safer now to move down from her secluded mountain shelter to Plankerhof, a hamlet near Frassilongo, to a flat beside the church.
    现在，从她那隔绝人烟的山间住所搬到Frassilongo附近的小村庄Plankerhof，搬到教堂旁边的一间公寓，觉得更安全了。

    secluded 隔绝的，隐居的

    hamlet 小村庄

  2. In the ideal version of American politics, economic-recovery legislation would be proportionate to crisis indicators, not the partisan affiliations of elected officeholders.
    美国政治的理想版本为经济复苏立法应该与危机指数成比例，而与民选官员的党派关系没关联。

    crisis indicators 危机指标，具体什么意思不知道，也找不到

    partisan affiliations 党派关系

  3. Italy’s capital markets are shallow compared with the rest of Europe, let alone America.
    与欧洲其他国家相比，意大利的资本市场很薄弱，更不用与美国相比了。

    let alone 更不用说

    shallow 薄弱的

  4. Between now and November 3rd, Floridians should prepare themselves for an onslaught of ads, attention and hand-wringing from the two campaigns.
    从现在到11月3日，佛罗里达人应该为两次竞选带来的广告、关注和绝望的一系列冲击做好准备。

    hand-wringing 绝望

    onslaught 猛攻，突击

  5. She was a Kashgar woman who, according to Chinese legend, enraptured the 18th-century Qianlong emperor with her mysterious natural scent, after being captured and delivered to his harem.
    据中国传说，她是一名喀什女子，在被抓获并送往乾隆后宫后，她那神秘的天然香味令乾隆皇帝心醉神迷。

    enraptured 狂喜的；著迷的

    harem 后宫

    可能这就是<书剑恩仇录>的香香公主本人吧
